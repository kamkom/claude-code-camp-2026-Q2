# World Map and Locations

## Midgaard City Area (SAFE ZONE - Recommended Level)
- **Tournament and Practice Yard** - Starting location with Guildmaster. Exits: north (bar), down (well)
- **Bar of Swordsmen** - Damaged furniture. Exits: south (yard), west (entrance hall). NPCs: waiter
- **Entrance Hall to Guild of Swordsmen** - Entrance to guild with ATM. Exits: north (main street), east (bar). NPCs: knight
- **Main Street** - Central hub. Exits: north (weapon shop), south (guild), east (leave town), west (market square). NPCs: Peacekeeper, 2x beastly fido

## Midgaard Outskirts & Forest (ABOVE RECOMMENDED LEVEL WARNING)
- **Inside East Gate of Midgaard** - Two small towers and footbridge. Exits: east (outside gate), south (water shop), west (main street). NPCs: 4x cityguards, 1x oozing green blob
- **Outside East Gate of Midgaard** - Plains stretch to east. Exits: east (City Entrance), west (inside gate)
- **City Entrance** - Outskirts of Midgaard. Exits: east (forest/laneway), west (main street)
- **The Laneway** - Dense trees. Exits: north (hills trail), east (forest), west (city), down (Dwarven Village path)

## Mountain Areas (ABOVE RECOMMENDED LEVEL - DANGEROUS)
- **The Lane** - Pleasant shady lane. Exits: north (plains), south (laneway)
- **The Plains** - Vast desolate area with small path to hills. Exits: north (hills), east (hillside), south (lane)
- **A Scraggly Trail** - Rocky ground with sign (readable?). Exits: east (valley trail), west (plains)
- **A Scraggly Trail (valley)** - Slopes up with gray stone. House visible below. Exits: east, down
- **A Groomed Path** - Very small valley with cottage. Exits: east (house), up (mountain)
- **Outside A House** - Path from cottage door to valley edge. Something wrong here. Exits: east (smithery), west (groomed path)

## Destroyed/Cursed Areas (INVESTIGATE CAREFULLY)
- **The Smithery** - DEVASTATED. Forge blasted apart, hammers/tongs scattered, bed smoulders, burnt cadaver on ground. Exits: east (garden), west (house)
- **A Small Garden** - Blasted by fireball. Nothing but blast-marks and ashes. Exits: east (ridge), west (smithery)
- **Mountain Ridge** - View of cottage in valley below. Sheer drops north/west. Exits: south (mountain ridge), west (ledge down)
- **The Mountain Ridge** - Ridge continues north. Small plateau to south. Bad vibes from dark peak. Exits: north (high ridge), south (diverging paths)
- **The Diverging Paths** - Plateau on mountains. Multiple slopes down. Ominous dark peak to west. Exits: north, east, south, west
- **A Ledge** - Overlooks path. Cottage below with smoke from ruins. Dark peak above. Boot-tracks lead down. Exits: east, up, down
- **Outside Of A Cottage** - Gutted ruins, no chimney smoke. Large hole in wall. Exits: west (into ruin), up (ledge)
- **The Torched Cottage** - DESTROYED. Burnt and dessicated corpse of man on floor. Exits: east (outside)

## Treacherous Areas
- **A Rocky Slope** (west side) - Shallow slope, loose shale/gravel. Ominous dark peak overhead. Exits: east, west
- **A Steep Slope** - Leads down/west from plateau. Dangerous but climbable. Signs of traffic. Exits: north, west, up
- **A Small Plateau** - Bordered by rock wall north. Multiple exits. Exits: east, south, west
- **A Small Plateau** (end) - Plateau ends with drop-off south, rock wall north. Possible cave south/down. Exits: east, west
- **A Rocky Slope** (treacherous) - Very dangerous, shale underfoot. Only safe route north. Exits: north, east
- **The Overlook** - Point outcropping with beautiful mountain view. Bad vibes from dark peak. Only safe exit south. Exits: south
