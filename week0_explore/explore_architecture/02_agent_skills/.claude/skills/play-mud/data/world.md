# World Map

## Midgaard

### East Gate Area
- **Outside The East Gate Of Midgaard** - By two towers connected with footbridge across heavy wooden gate
  - Exits: east (plains), west (inside gate)
  
- **Inside The East Gate Of Midgaard** - Inside the east gate, guards stationed here
  - Exits: east (outside), south (Water Shop), west (Main Street)
  - NPCs: Peacekeeper, cityguards (4x)

### Main Street
- **Main Street** - Central street through Midgaard
  - Exits: north (weapon shop), south (Guild of Swordsmen), east (gate), west (market square)
  - NPCs: Peacekeeper, beastly fido (2x)

### Guild of Swordsmen
- **The Entrance Hall To The Guild Of Swordsmen** - Guild entrance
  - Exits: north (main street), east (bar)
  - NPCs: knight (guard), oozing blob
  - Objects: automatic teller machine

- **The Bar Of Swordsmen** - Damaged bar area
  - Exits: south (yard), west (entrance hall)
  - NPCs: waiter
  - Objects: large bulletin board

- **The Tournament And Practice Yard** - Practice area for fighters
  - Exits: north (bar), down (well leading to darkness)
  - NPCs: guildmaster (sharpening axe)
  - **Skills available**: kick practice
